Unlock the Power of QR Codes with linktoQR

Welcome to linktoQR, your ultimate solution for seamless QR code generation and scanning. Our platform is designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind, providing you with all the tools you need to interact with QR codes effortlessly.

How to Use linktoQR:

1. Generate QR Codes:

Creating your own QR code is just a few clicks away:

  • Click on the "Generate QR" button on the homepage.
  • In the "Enter Link" field, type or paste the URL you want to encode. We support various formats including:
    • www.yourwebsite.com
    • yourwebsite.com
    • https://www.yourwebsite.com
  • Click the "Generate QR" button.
  • Your QR code will be instantly generated and displayed. You'll also get a "Download QR Code" button to save it as a PNG image.
  • If you enter a link in an unsupported format, an error message will guide you to the correct format.

2. Scan QR Codes:

Scanning QR codes is quick and easy with linktoQR:

  • Click on the "Scan QR" button on the homepage.
  • You have two options for scanning:
    • Scan with Camera: Click the "Scan with Camera" button. Your device will request camera access. Once granted, point your camera at the QR code you want to scan. The scanned result will appear automatically.
    • Upload QR Code: Click on the "Choose File" button and select an image file containing a QR code from your device. The platform will process the image and display the scanned result.
  • Once the QR code is scanned, the decoded information (usually a website link or text) will be displayed in the "Scan Result" area.
  • A "Copy Result" button will appear, allowing you to easily copy the scanned information to your clipboard.
